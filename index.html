Find a friend for life

When pre-loved toys need a new home, they come to us.
We help them find a forever home with you so that you can make a new friend for life.

We know each toy is different, just like us!
That's why we help you find the perfect fit for you and your home.

Each toy comes with an adoption pack, including a certificate of adoption, and how to take care of your new friend.

Meet Our Featured Friends

Meet our Featured Friends

Click on them to find out more about them!

Bella

Type: Bunny

Age: 4 years old

Bella is one of the cutest bunnies around! She is very talkative and loves a good story.

As you can probably tell, her favourite colour is yellow, just like the sun! This is because she loves going outside.

In fact, one of her favourite things to do on a nice sunny day is to go searching for wonderfully coloured flowers!

With her positive energy and charming smile, Bella will always be there if you need somebody to talk to.

Bradley

Type: Bear

Age: 15 years old

Bradley is one smart guy! He is very kind and likes to help people with their problems.

He always tries his best to find a solution to every problem, and to make things better he is a great listener!

One thing you should know about Bradley is that he will be there for you when times get tough.

He always carries with him a big optimistic smile, and he loves a nice long hug.

Goldie

Type: Golden Retriever

Age: 64 years old

If there is anything that Goldie loves, it would have to be a good long nap.

He loves spending his days lounging around the house, cuddling up on the couch, and sleeping huddled up in bed.

He loves being stroked under the chin and behind his cute floppy ears.

One thing you should know is that with Goldie around you will never be lonely.

He is as loyal a dog, which means he will always be there for you, no matter what!

Harrison

Type: Hare

Age: 8 years old

If anyone likes to play, it’s Harrison!

He has a heart of gold and loves to hop around outside and roll around in the long grass.

His favourite thing to do is to go exploring, and when he grows up, he wants to be an adventurer, just like his grandfather!

Harrison is a fantastic companion and no matter where you take him, you can trust that he will always be there, enthusiastically by your side!

Kenzie

Type: Kookaburra

Age: 57 years old

Kenzie has to be one of the wisest birds we know!

She is always full of great advice and will always find a solution when times get tricky.

With her fluffy personality, comes an even fluffier exterior!

She is one of the softest and sweetest birds in the entire world, which means you will never want to be without her by your side.

She loves digging her beak into a nice book alongside a nice warm cup of tea.

Percy

Type: Poodle

Age: 32 years old

When it comes to getting your hands dirty, Percy is your guy!

His most favourite thing to do is gardening, he loves spending his time in the garden growing all sorts of things from pumpkins to avocados!

After a long day of gardening, this little environmentalist likes to show off his cooking skills by making food with the produce he grows.

His food is loved by all, and he makes a pretty mean vegetable lasagna.

Sally

Type: Ragdoll

Age: 6 years old

Meet Sally! The world's kindest sweetheart… literally!

If there is one thing Sally loves it is candy, in fact, cotton candy and sprinkles are her favourites.

Aside from her love for candy, she also likes to spend time at the park, her favourite section would have to be the swings.

Another reason why she loves the park is that she likes to meet new people, and her bubbly personality means that she gets along with everyone she meets!

If you want a new best friend, Sally is the perfect candidate.

Todd

Type: Turtle

Age: 5 years old

Todd dreams of one day becoming a world-class racer!

You may find him whizzing around the house, practising for his next race.

He may not be fast, but he is a dreamer. He always tries his best and that is just one of Todd’s many great qualities!

On his days off of the racecourse, he likes to relax.

His favourite position to be in is on his back with his legs in the air! He loves a good tummy rub and lots of attention.
